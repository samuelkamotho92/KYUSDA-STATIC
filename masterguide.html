About Master Guide

Role of Master Guide Depertement

Master Guide Depertment is responsible for development and Mentoring Program for youth Leadership within the seventh-day Adventist Church,serving the entire community

Purpose

Develop Christ-Like Leaders to discipline children,youth and Young Adults

Mission

To empower leaders through effective Training,equipping and Deploying them for service

Master Guide Plege

Loving the Lord Jesus,I promise to take an active part in work of the Adventurer,Pathfinder and Youth ministries ,doing what I can to help finish the work of the Gospel in all the world

Law

The Master Guide Law Calls me to:be an example in World,Conduct,Love,Spirit,Faith,And Purity

Based on 1st Timothy 4:12
