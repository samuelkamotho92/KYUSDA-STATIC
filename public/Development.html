About Development

Development and welfare department is a two-in-one department that deals with both the general development of the church and the welfare of it's members.The development part focuses mainly on the physical growth of the church,it fosters growth through finding ways in which to fund and boost the church Treasury,through the grand contributions we have annually,the department also helps to boost many other departments so as to make the programs of the church run smoothly financially. The welfare part fosters the church(members) bond through socialization activities like the trips and Social Sundays.Through this,members get to interact with each other more and hence promoting brotherhood and sisterhood in Christ.The welfare department also deals with individual wellbeing of the members, through it's kitty,it helps those members who are of need and no where to lean on. The two departments are of essential benefit to both the church development activities and the members wellbeing.
