In These Last Days: The Message of Hebrews

LESSON 3 DISCUSSION

The Promised Son

Read For this Week's Study
Isa 2:2, Heb.1:4, Exd.24:16, Isa. 44:24, Heb 1:10, Luke 1:31,32. Heb 1:5.

Memory Text

“In these last days, he talked to us through [by] his Son. God chose him to be the one to whom [the one who] he would give all things. God also made the world [the earth and everything on it] by this Son. He [the Son] shines as bright as God. He is just like [the same as] God himself. He holds up the world by the power of his word. He made people clean from their wrong ways. Then he sat down beside [next to] God in heaven”.
(Hebrews 1:2, 3)

Discussion Questions
  • When we understand better what Jesus said and did, we will understand God better. How has your understanding of Jesus helped your walk with God?
  • The love that God showed Jesus is the love that God wants to show us. What should that Bible truth tell us about how we should love other people, too?
  • The love that God showed Jesus is the love that God wants to show us. What should that Bible truth tell us about how we should love other people, too?
  • How is our giving glory to God part of the Three Angels’ Messages in Revelation 14:7 and present truth? Present truth is the Bible truth we need most for our time

LESSON 4 DISCUSSION

Jesus, Our Obedient Brother

Read For this Week's Study
Leviticus 25:25–27, 47–49; Hebrews 11:24–26; 1 Corinthians 15:50; Hebrews 12:1–4..

Memory Text

: “God’s children all have a body. That is why Jesus himself had the same [body]. But because Jesus also had a body, he was able to die and stop the devil. The devil has power to make people die. But Jesus, by his own death, was able to stop the power of death”
(Hebrews 2:14).

Discussion Questions
  • Hebrews tells us that Jesus became our Brother to save us. Knowing this, what do you think when you see people refuse to accept His mercy and be saved?
  • How does the story about Moses help us understand, in a small way, what Jesus did for us and continues to do now?
