About Prophecy Depertment

The Spirit of Prophecy Department is among the departments in the Seventh day Adventist Church which promotes and encourages believers to read and be well Conversant with the testimony of Jesus ( Rev 19:10) as expressed by Ellen .G. White. It also promotes Bible prophecy as revealed in the two major prophetic books; Daniel and Revelation in connection with the three angels' message for the preparation of every nation, and kindred, and tongue, and people for the glorious second coming of Christ .
